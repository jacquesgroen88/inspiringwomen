Guilt-Free Chocolate Brownies - Low Carb & Keto

Lisa B. March 18, 2026

Indulge Without the Sugar Crash

Satisfy your intense sweet tooth without the guilt with these delicious low carb chocolate brownies. They are incredibly fudgey, rich, completely gluten-free, and perfectly keto-friendly.

Ingredients You Will Need:

  • 1 cup Super-Fine Almond Flour
  • 1/4 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder (High Quality)
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Flour
  • 1/2 cup Erythritol, Xylitol, or your preferred keto granular sweetener
  • 1/2 cup Grass-Fed Butter (Melted)
  • 3 Large Free-Range Eggs
  • 1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: Sugar-free chocolate chips or chopped walnuts

Baking Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together all the dry ingredients (almond flour, cocoa, coconut flour, sweetener) to remove any lumps.
  3. Stir in the melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix until a smooth, thick batter forms. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the edges are set but the center is still slightly soft.
  5. Let them cool completely in the pan before cutting—this is the hardest part, but it ensures they don't crumble!
