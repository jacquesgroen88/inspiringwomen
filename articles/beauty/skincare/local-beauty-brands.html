Affordable Local Beauty Brands You Need to Try

Chloe S. April 26, 2026

Local is Lekker: Top SA Skincare Finds for 2026

Why import expensive, highly-taxed international skincare when South Africa boasts some incredible, high-quality, and deeply affordable local beauty brands? Don't make the skincare mistake of ignoring these homegrown gems!

1. Standard Beauty

Known for their potent active ingredients and incredibly accessible price point. Their serums—especially the 10% Niacinamide and Hyaluronic Acid—rival top international brands like The Ordinary. They are vegan and cruelty-free.

2. SKOON. Skin

Clean, natural, and highly effective. SKOON focuses on non-toxic, locally sourced ingredients (like Rooibos and Buchu) that deliver real clinical results. Their Retinin night cream is a must-try for anti-aging.

3. Lulu & Marula

Small-batch, all-natural skincare that feels luxurious. Their purifying cleansing balms and balancing body oils smell divine and leave the skin feeling deeply nourished and glowing.

By purchasing from these local brands, you get amazing, scientifically-backed products while actively supporting local female entrepreneurs and boosting the SA economy. It's a complete win-win!
