How To Manage Your Finances in 2024

Thandiwe N. January 5, 2026

Taking Control of Your Money

Managing finances in South Africa requires strategic planning, especially with rising inflation and interest rates. Here is how you can take control of your financial future.

1. Track Every Rand

You can't manage what you don't measure. Use a budgeting app or a simple spreadsheet to track your income and expenses. You'll be surprised where your money goes!

2. Build an Emergency Fund

Aim to save at least 3-6 months of living expenses. This fund is strictly for emergencies—like a burst geyser or unexpected medical bills—not for a holiday or a wedding (see our Wedding Budget Guide).

3. Pay Off High-Interest Debt

Credit card debt and personal loans eat away at your wealth. Focus on paying these off using the avalanche or snowball method.

4. Start a Side Hustle

If you need extra income to reach your goals, consider starting a small business or exploring our Top 5 Side Hustles for SA Women.
