Guilt-Free Chocolate Doughnuts - Low Carb!

Lisa B. March 20, 2026

The Ultimate Keto Treat

These delicious chocolate doughnuts are low carb and keto-friendly. Perfect for a sweet treat that won't derail your diet.

If you loved our Keto Brownies, you will be obsessed with these baked doughnuts.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Almond Flour
  • 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1/3 cup Erythritol
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 cup Melted Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Unsweetened Almond Milk

Instructions:

Mix dry ingredients. Whisk wet ingredients separately, then combine. Pour batter into a greased doughnut pan and bake at 175°C for 15 minutes. Let cool before adding a sugar-free chocolate glaze!
